I am a post-doc at MIT. Previously, I was a post-doc at the Euler International Mathematical Institute. Before that, I was a graduate student of Pavel Safronov at the University of Zurich.
[I would like to thank the Department of Mathematics of MIT for their hospitality after anti-war demonstration in Russia.]

My research interests lie on the intersection of representation theory, quantum groups, and algebraic geometry. Currently, I am studying the relations between infinite-dimensional quantum groups (Yangians, quantum affine algebras), (q)W-algebras, and nonstandard quantizations of GL(N).
